Making A Difference

The mission of Care For Humanity is to help and assist people in need. Provide Aid to vulnerable less fortunate communities in the world. Provide clean drinking water to millions who don’t have access to this basic human need. Care of Humanity also raises funds to help: Orphans, Widows and the less fortunate throughout our global community. Care For Humanity will also participate in raising funds for worldwide catastrophes and International Humanitarian Aid and Relief. Funds raised for the international effort will be channeled through US Based Charities with operations overseas.

We are People from all different walks of life working together to make a difference! Think globally, act locally!
Care For humanity operates at seven percent administrative cost! Which means 93% of all donations go to people in need!

Clean Water Projects

Care for Humanity provides clean drinking water by building water wells in places that are in real need of services such as Uganda, Tanzania, Kenya, Madagascar, Pakistan and Bangladesh.

Donate Now!

Clean Drinking Water Project in Uganda

Care for Humanity has built 52 water wells in Uganda in Mbale, Masindi and Karamoja District. 1 water well can serve up to 200 people from neighboring villages

Clean Drinking Water Project in Tanzania

Care For Humanity has built 13 water wells in Tanzania. 1 water well will service over 200 people in the poor remote villages

Clean Drinking Water Well Project in Kenya

Care for Humanity has built 4 Water Wells in Kenya. 1 Water well will serve over 200 people in poor remote village area.

Clean Drinking Water in Madagascar

Care For Humanity has built 22 water wells in Madagascar

Clean Drinking Water in Pakistan

Care For Humanity has built 140 water pumps in remote rural villages in Sindh Pakistan plus 3 big water wells and 3 solar water well tanks.

Clean Drinking Water in Bangladesh

Clean Water in Bangladesh – Water Pumps Helping the Rohingya Refugees in Cox Bazar
